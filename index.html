Atakan Tuzcu

Atakan Tuzcu, was born in 30 October 1995, in İzmir, Türkiye. He started elementary school at 12 Eylül İlköğretim Okulu but he transferred to Uğur Mumcu İlköğretim Okulu in Kocaeli, Türkiye at 3rd grade. After graduating from primary school in 2009, he went to high school at Cihat Kora Anadolu Lisesi. He graduated from high school in 2014 and he started Computer Engineering major at Gediz University, in İzmir,Türkiye. He transferred to Yeditepe Unitersity but he took classes from Dokuz Eylül University as a special student. After finishing his bachelor's degree he started master's degree at University of Bakırçay. He graduated at 2024.

He started his professional life at Netle Software as Software Engineer. After 9 months, he changed his company to Veripark Software as Software Engineer. Now he is still working as Software Test Engineer in Veripark Software.
